I'm having a fanart contest! Here are the details:

How to Enter

In order to participate in the contest, you must draw one of the characters I have designed. You can download some art of these characters here and/or watch some videos featuring them on my YouTube. There are no other restrictions, but please put a decent amount of effort into your submission to be fair to the other entrants (it doesn't have to be "good", but don't be lazy).

You can submit your entry by mentioning me on Twitter (with your submission attached), sharing it in the #contest channel on the official Discord server, or sending an email to contest@carbon.moe. Please also let me know how you would like to be credited. You may submit multiple entries to be considered for the first prize set, but you can only have a maximum of 1 entry for the second prize set.

Contest End

The contest will end January 1st, 2019 and no new entries will be accepted beyond that date. There will be a YouTube stream within 30 days to display the entries and announce the winners.

Miscellaneous Details

  • All entrants retain ownership of their submitted artwork, however they agree to allow me to show their work on stream and on social media.
  • YouTube does not endorse and is in no way connected to this contest. Entries must comply with YouTube's community guidelines.
  • Privacy Notice: Your artwork and contact information will be retained until all prizes have been delivered. If you are a winner, I will ask you for your shipping address. After your prize is shipped I will delete it.
  • If you do not claim your prize within 7 days of the announcement of the winners (and my attempts to contact you), then you forfeit your prize and a new winner will be chosen.
  • Prizes will be shipped within 30 days of the announcement of the winners and procurement of shipping information.
  • This contest makes every effort to comply with all applicable laws and guidelines. If there is an oversight, please email contest@carbon.moe and it will be corrected. If this contest would not be legal where you live, then you cannot participate.
